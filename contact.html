Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the brand transformation process take?

The timeline varies based on your goals and starting point. Most clients see significant results within 3-6 months of consistent implementation. Our Professional package typically includes a 3-month roadmap, while the Elite package extends to 6 months for more comprehensive transformation.

How is WhatsApp TV different from regular WhatsApp?

WhatsApp TV is a strategic approach to using WhatsApp broadcast lists and groups to deliver valuable content to your audience on a consistent schedule, similar to a TV channel. It includes content planning, audience segmentation, and monetization strategies that transform a simple messaging app into a powerful broadcasting platform for your brand.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Abe works with clients worldwide through virtual coaching sessions, WhatsApp consultations, and digital resources. All services are available remotely, and we accommodate different time zones for your convenience.

What makes your approach to personal branding unique?

Abe's approach is unique because it integrates visual design, digital marketing, and relationship psychology. While most brand coaches focus solely on visuals or marketing, Abe recognizes that authentic relationships are the foundation of a powerful personal brand. This holistic methodology creates brands that not only look good but also forge meaningful connections.

How do I know which service is right for me?

The best way to determine the right service for your needs is to book a free 30-minute consultation with Coach Pride . During this call, he'll assess your current brand position, understand your goals, and recommend the most effective path forward.
