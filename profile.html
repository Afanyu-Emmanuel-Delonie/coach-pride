FitLife Rebrand

Graphic Design 2022

The Challenge

FitLife, a growing fitness coaching business, was struggling with an outdated brand identity that didn't reflect their modern approach to wellness. Their logo, marketing materials, and social media presence lacked cohesion and failed to attract their target demographic of young professionals.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive brand identity system that included a dynamic new logo, a vibrant color palette, custom typography, and a consistent visual language. The rebrand focused on conveying energy, transformation, and accessibility while maintaining a premium feel.

The Results

  • 42% increase in new client inquiries within the first month
  • Social media engagement up by 78%
  • Brand recognition improved significantly in target market surveys
  • Successfully launched new premium service tier at 30% higher price point
Abe completely transformed our brand from looking like a small local gym to a premium fitness lifestyle brand. The investment in our rebrand paid for itself within the first three months through increased client acquisition and retention.
Michael Torres Founder, FitLife

Fashion Forward WhatsApp TV Channel

WhatsApp TV 2023

The Challenge

Fashion Forward, an emerging fashion boutique, was struggling to build a loyal customer base and generate consistent sales. Traditional marketing channels were proving too expensive, and their social media efforts weren't converting to actual purchases.

The Solution

We created a WhatsApp TV channel strategy that delivered exclusive content directly to customers' phones. The channel featured daily outfit inspirations, behind-the-scenes looks at new arrivals, limited-time offers, and styling tips. We implemented a content calendar, audience segmentation, and engagement tactics to maximize effectiveness.

The Results

  • Built a subscriber base of 3,200+ active viewers in 6 months
  • 93% open rate on WhatsApp broadcasts (compared to 22% email open rate)
  • Generated 500, 000 XAF in direct sales from WhatsApp-exclusive offers
  • Reduced marketing costs by 62% while increasing conversion rates
The WhatsApp TV channel Abe created has completely revolutionized how we connect with customers. It's intimate, immediate, and incredibly effective. Our customers feel like they're getting VIP access, and our sales have never been better.
Sophia Chen Owner, Fashion Shop owner

Beauty Influencer Instagram Growth

Social Media 2023

The Challenge

Maria Rodriguez, a talented makeup artist, had been trying to build her Instagram presence for over a year with minimal results. Despite creating quality content, she was stuck at 2,300 followers with low engagement and struggling to attract brand partnerships.

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive Instagram growth strategy that included content restructuring, strategic hashtag research, engagement tactics, and a consistent posting schedule. We also developed a unique visual style that helped her stand out in the crowded beauty space and created a content pillars system to ensure variety while maintaining brand consistency.

The Results

  • Grew from 2,300 to 27,500 followers in 6 months
  • Engagement rate increased from 1.2% to 5.8%
  • Secured 5 paid brand partnerships worth 350,000 XAF
  • Launched a successful online makeup course generating 300,000 XAF
I was about to give up on my dream of being a beauty influencer when I found Abe. His strategies completely transformed my Instagram presence and turned my passion into a profitable career. The investment in his services was the best business decision I've ever made.
Maria Rodriguez Beauty Influencer
