About Coach Pride

The story, philosophy, and vision behind the brand

The Journey

Abe Pride's path to becoming a leading personal brand coach began over 15 years ago when he recognized a critical gap in the market: many talented professionals struggled to effectively communicate their value and build a recognizable brand.

After experiencing his own challenges in establishing his identity in the competitive graphic design industry, Abe developed a unique methodology that combines visual storytelling, strategic marketing, and authentic relationship building.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into a comprehensive brand coaching practice that has transformed the lives and businesses of over 500 clients worldwide.

Your personal brand is the story people tell about you when you're not in the room. My mission is to help you write that story.

My Philosophy

Authenticity First

Your brand must be a genuine reflection of who you are. Authenticity creates trust, and trust builds lasting relationships.

Strategic Visibility

Being seen by the right people, in the right places, with the right message is the foundation of effective personal branding.

Relationship-Centered

Strong brands are built on strong relationships. Every interaction is an opportunity to strengthen your brand connection.

Continuous Evolution

Your brand should grow as you grow. Embracing change while maintaining your core values creates a dynamic, resilient brand.
